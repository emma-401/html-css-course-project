Taco Trivia

When did taco first appear in the United States?

Tacos first appeared in the United States in the early 20th century, though the exact date is a bit fuzzy. Mexican immigrants brought them with them, especially in Texas and California, and they started appearing in U.S. cities around the 1910s–1920s
